AQI kya hota hai? Yeh number samajhna hai toh yaha click karo 👇

AQI (Air Quality Index) ek 0 se 500 tak ka number hai jo batata hai hawa mein kitna pollution hai — jitna zyada number, utni kharab hawa. Yeh number hawa mein present PM2.5, PM10, ozone jaise pollutants ko naap kar banaya jaata hai, jaise thermometer body ka temperature naapta hai.

Ye app kis liye banaya gaya hai?

Hawa dikhti nahi, isliye usko ignore karna aasan hai — jab tak ki wo already kharab na ho chuki ho. Station/AQI is banaya gaya hai taaki har koi, chahe wo AQI ke baare mein kuch jaanta ho ya bilkul naya ho, ek nazar mein samajh sake ki aaj bahar ki hawa kaisi hai — aur uske hisaab se apna din plan kar sake.
